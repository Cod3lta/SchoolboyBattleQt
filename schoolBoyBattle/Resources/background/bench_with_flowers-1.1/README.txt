Name:
  Bench with Flowers
Version:
  1.1
Description:
  Benches with flowers tilesets created for Stendhal.
Details:
  - Orientation: orthogonal
  - Designed for use with 32x32 tiled maps.
  - PNG images use indexed color (smaller files).
  - Images with more than 256 colors & GIMP sources (.xcf) use RGB color.
